Expertise My Expertise & Skills

Expert services in physics research and data science

With over a decade of research experience and expertise spanning experimental physics, computational methods, and advanced data analysis, I offer comprehensive services tailored to research institutions, companies, and organizations seeking specialized expertise in materials characterization and scientific problem-solving.

Coherent X-Ray Diffraction

Expert in time-resolved coherent x-ray diffraction and Bragg Coherent Diffraction Imaging (BCDI) for strain imaging of crystalline materials. Extensive experience designing and executing sophisticated scattering experiments at synchrotron beamlines.

  • Bragg Coherent Diffraction Imaging
  • Strain Field Reconstruction
  • Synchrotron Beamline Operations

Electron Microscopy & Diffraction

Specialized expertise in coherent electron beam diffraction (CBED), transmission electron microscopy (TEM), and resolution estimation of electron diffraction data from single-protein molecules. Proficient in quantitative analysis of diffraction patterns.

  • Convergent Beam Electron Diffraction
  • Single-Particle Imaging
  • Diffraction Pattern Analysis

Python & Scientific Computing

Advanced Python proficiency for scientific data analysis, visualization, and computational workflows. Expertise with PyTorch, machine learning algorithms, optimization methods, and large-scale dataset management.

  • Data Analysis & Visualization
  • Machine Learning (PyTorch)
  • Optimization Algorithms

Materials Characterization

Comprehensive expertise in characterizing ferroelectric materials, quantum dots, heterostructures, and nanowires. Skilled in x-ray reflectivity, Laue diffraction, and computational modeling of scattering phenomena.

  • Ferroelectric Materials
  • Quantum Dot Heterostructures
  • X-Ray Reflectivity

Experimental Design & Instrumentation

Proficient in designing complex experimental campaigns, commissioning and calibrating sophisticated instrumentation, and optimizing beamline setups for maximum scientific impact. Experience with multi-year collaborative projects.
